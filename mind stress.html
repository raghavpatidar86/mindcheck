💡 Tips

Stress Management Tips

Simple, effective strategies to reduce stress and improve your mental well-being every day.

🌿

Deep Breathing

Practice 4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4 sec, hold 7, exhale 8. Repeat 4 times daily to calm your nervous system.

🏃

Exercise Daily

Even a 20-minute walk boosts endorphins and significantly reduces cortisol — your stress hormone.

🛏️

Sleep Hygiene

Keep a consistent sleep schedule. Avoid screens 1 hour before bed. Aim for 7-9 hours every night.

🧘

Mindfulness

Spend 10 minutes daily in meditation. Apps like Headspace or Calm can help you get started.

👥

Social Support

Talk to someone you trust. Sharing your feelings reduces emotional burden and strengthens bonds.

📵

Digital Detox

Set screen-free hours each day. Social media overload is a major hidden stressor for most people.
